Frequently Asked Questions

HOW ARE YOU DIFFERENT FROM OTHER HOTEL BOOKING APPS/WEBSITES?

We are a localized distribution channel based in Singapore. People who are geographically distanced from Singapore are unable to book hotel rooms through our app. Why should locals who want to go on staycations compete with travellers for the same room? Following this logic, we are thus able to offer exclusive prices for locals, giving you a greater value for your buck.

Why can you provide such low prices?

Shhh, this is our trade-secret. Rest assured, it’s by all means legal.

...Really? How can you ensure that you have the lowest price guarantee?

We compare these prices with the lowest price on the Internet, and guarantee that we have the lowest prices just for you. There’s just no need to worry when you’re booking through our platform - we do all the work for you!

What devices does your app support?

Our app currently supports iOS, but trust us when we say that our tech team is working hard to get the Android version published!

Which payment options can I choose?

You can pay via credit or debit card. We’re also happy to accept some additional love!

Will the hotel still impose additional charges?

Rest assured. The hotel will not charge you for the room as long as you have paid for it on our app - unless you order that delicious creme brulee via room service.

Why are the range of hotels so limited on the app?

We are pretty new in the market, which is why it’s challenging to get numerous hotels in a small period of time. Please continue to believe in us as we gather hotels!

Can I cancel my reservation?

Nope, once you’ve paid for a room on our app, you cannot cancel your reservation, nor will you get any refund.

Can I change my booking date?

The booking date is fixed once you have paid for a room on our app. However, if you have yet to pay for it, you can go back in the app to change your staycation dates!

How do I book a room?

Simple. Register, choose your booking dates and hotel, then pay! You can also refer here for instructions.

How long does it take to book a room?

Hmm, maybe less than 3 minutes. It’s that simple. Unless you can’t decide between that fancy hotel or the hipster boutique!
